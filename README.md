Ghost of the Mead Hall
Ghost of the Mead Hall is a Valheim mod that adds a spectral presence to your home. When your hearth is warm and your comfort is high, a restless spirit may appear to haunt your halls.

Features
Restless Spirit: A ghost will occasionally spawn inside your shelter at night.

Comfort Requirement: The spirit only appears for players who have achieved a Comfort Level of 15 or higher.

Atmospheric: Designed to add mystery and lore to high-tier player bases.

Installation
Ensure you have BepInEx Pack for Valheim installed.

Download GhostMeadHall.dll.

Place the .dll file into your Valheim plugins folder:
Steam/steamapps/common/Valheim/BepInEx/plugins/

Launch Valheim and reach a Comfort Level of 15+ in a sheltered area at night.

Requirements
Valheim (Steam Version)

BepInEx Pack for Valheim

Technical Details
The mod checks for the following conditions before attempting a spawn:

Time: Must be Night.

Environment: Player must be in a "Sheltered" state.

Comfort: Player must have a minimum Comfort Level of 15.

Troubleshooting:
If the Ghost of the Mead Hall is not appearing as expected, please verify the following conditions:

Comfort Level: Ensure your character's current Comfort Level is exactly 15 or higher. You can check this by looking at the "Rested" buff icon in the top right of your screen.

Shelter Requirement: You must be standing in a location the game recognizes as "Sheltered" (under a roof and protected from the elements).

Time of Day: The ghost will only attempt to spawn during the Night cycle.

Plugin Check: Verify that GhostMeadHall.dll is located in:
Steam/steamapps/common/Valheim/BepInEx/plugins/

Log Check: Open the BepInEx console (or check the LogOutput.log file) to ensure the mod has loaded without assembly errors.
